Introduction to Trusted Tester Methods Workshop

ICT Accessibility Testing Symposium - 2019

A one-day workshop intended to provide an overview of the Trusted Tester process, review of some of the key test steps, and some tips and tricks for progressing through the Trusted Tester training course and certification.

Workshop Goals

  • Provide an understanding of the Trusted Tester certification course
  • Introduce you to use of the ANDI tool
  • Give you hands-on experience testing using ANDI
  • Provide testing tips and tricks
  • Expose you to some of the FAQ
